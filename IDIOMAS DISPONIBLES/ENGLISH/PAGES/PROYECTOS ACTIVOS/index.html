With an alarming decrease in precipitation and depletion of water resources, the drought is affecting both the population and the natural and economic environment of the area.

Thanks to your support, we have created a voting system. What is it about? Now we explain:

Our voting system in the sustainability organization is designed to ensure fair participation and democratic decision-making. Each member is entitled to cast a vote on key decisions affecting our mission and objectives. We value transparency and inclusion, and encourage informed debate to reach consensus that reflects the values and goals of our community. Together, we make every vote count in our commitment to a more sustainable and prosperous future for all.

Example of how the votes would look


Project Upvotes Downvotes

Reducing Pollution 4 3
Regulating Deforestation 7 9

We value diverse opinions and strive to achieve consensus that reflects the shared values and goals of our community. Together, we build a more sustainable future through an inclusive and collaborative voting system. Anything you would like to add, send us an email ;).
