Why Support Farmers?


They're Super Necessary! Let Us Explain Why!

Supporting farmers is essential because they are responsible for growing the food we consume daily. Their work is crucial to ensuring food security and access to nutritious food for the entire population. Additionally, supporting farmers contributes to the economic development of rural communities, promotes sustainable agricultural practices, and helps conserve the environment. In summary, providing support to farmers not only benefits them and their families but also has a positive impact on society as a whole.


Challenges They Face

Currently, farmers in Spain face a series of significant challenges. These problems include the volatility of agricultural product prices, which often do not cover production costs. Additionally, unfair competition from imported products from other countries and excessive bureaucracy make it difficult for agricultural operations to be profitable. Lack of access to water resources and the effects of climate change, such as droughts and extreme weather phenomena, also negatively affect agricultural production. These challenges require comprehensive solutions and policies that support farmers and promote sustainability in the agricultural sector in Spain.


What Can We Do to Help Them?


Buy Local Products

Buying local products is a powerful way to support farmers and strengthen communities. By opting for products grown or made near home, we directly contribute to the local economy by generating employment and fostering the development of family businesses and agricultural cooperatives. Additionally, by buying locally, we reduce the carbon footprint associated with transporting food long distances, which helps mitigate climate change and promotes more sustainable agricultural practices. Likewise, by preferring local products, we value the diversity and quality of food, preserving culinary traditions and promoting responsible agriculture. In summary, by choosing local products, we not only enjoy fresh and quality food but also contribute to the well-being of farmers and the sustainable development of our communities.

Support Them Through Demonstrations

Joining demonstrations with farmers is essential to show solidarity and support for a crucial sector of our society. By uniting in protest, we not only demonstrate our commitment to social and economic justice but also highlight the importance of protecting local agriculture and promoting policies that favor farmers. Our collective voice can influence governmental and corporate decision-making, advocating for fair working conditions, fair prices for agricultural products, and agricultural policies that promote sustainability and equity. Additionally, by joining demonstrations with farmers, we show appreciation for their hard work and stand in solidarity with their struggles for a fairer and more prosperous future for all. In summary, by uniting in demonstrations with farmers, we are defending values of justice, solidarity, and sustainability that benefit society as a whole.
